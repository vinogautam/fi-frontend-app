To assist you we may need to ask questions such as the ones listed below:

Financial Planning...???

  • What are your short, medium and long-term financial goals?
  • What type of pension do you currently have? Defined / Defined Contribution / Superannuation / RRSP / RRIF
  • Does anyone in your family have special needs, such as a disability, that will require special care?
  • What aspect of your financial situation are you most concerned with “Now”?
  • Do you have a financial plan in place?

Retirement Planning...???

  • What are your lifestyle goals for retirement?
  • If you were to die before your spouse, would you like to leave an estate large enough for your spouse to retire on?
  • If you are already retired, are you satisfied with your income and tax situation?

Insurance Planning...???

  • Could your family afford the lifestyle they currently enjoy in the event of your death?
  • Do you have your insurance needs professionally assessed, regularly?
  • Do you understand the effect inflation will have on your life insurance over time?

Business Planning…???...???

  • Are you a risk-oriented, conservative or somewhere in between investor?
  • What are your short, medium and long-term financial goals?
  • What level of certainty do have about your future income security, such as employment or pension income. (This can have a bearing on the type of investment advice given)
  • Do you contribute to RRSP’s regularly

Insurance Planning...???

  • Did you have a medical claim on this year’s personal tax return?
  • Did you have a medical claim on this year’s personal tax return?
  • Do you have a way to cope with the unexpected costs of critical illness?
  • Are you concerned that your inheritance goes to your beneficiaries and not to pay taxes?
  • Will you receive the maximum CPP at retirement?
  • Do you have retained earnings sitting idle in your company?
  • Do you qualify for income splitting?
  • Do you have a clear financial plan in place?
  • Could you increase your retirement assets with an Individual Pension Plan?
  • Is your business more or less profitable than when you last reviewed your will?
  • Do you plan any expansion?
  • Will you receive a retirement income from the business?
  • Will you sell the company when you retire? - What is/are your exit strategy(s)
  • Will you sell it to a family member or to a third party?
  • Do you plan to stay actively involved in your business when you retire?
  • Do you want to maintain ownership to provide income during retirement but not stay actively involved?

The questions noted above may or may not be asked exactly as shown. But questions requiring background information are generally required in order for our agents to answer your questions correctly.
